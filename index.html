About Us

Nurturing Tomorrow's Leaders Since 2009

Sri Kakatiya High School is a premier educational institution in Shadnagar, committed to holistic development of every child. We follow the Telangana SSC curriculum, blending academic rigor with value-based learning.

  • ✅ Telugu & English medium instruction available
  • ✅ State-board SSC curriculum (Nursery – Class X)
  • ✅ Child-friendly, safe & nurturing campus
  • ✅ Regular parent-teacher interaction programs
  • ✅ Focus on sports, arts & extracurricular activities
