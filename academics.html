Academic Overview

Education That Goes Beyond Textbooks

At Sri Kakatiya High School, academics is not just about scoring marks — it's about building understanding, developing skills, and igniting a lifelong passion for learning.

We follow the Telangana State Board SSC curriculum from Nursery through Class X, delivered through a blend of traditional teaching excellence and modern pedagogy. Every lesson is designed to be engaging, meaningful, and connected to the real world.

Our teaching team combines deep subject knowledge with genuine care for every student — adapting their approach to different learning styles and ensuring no child is left behind.

Library

A World of Books Awaits

Our school library is a haven for curious minds — a quiet, welcoming space where students can explore beyond their textbooks, develop a love for reading, and discover new worlds through literature.

The library houses a carefully curated collection of textbooks, reference books, story books, encyclopedias, periodicals, and digital learning materials — covering all subjects and suitable for all ages from primary to high school.

Dedicated library periods are scheduled each week, and students are encouraged to borrow books regularly. Our librarian actively guides students towards age-appropriate and enriching reading material.

Assessment System

Examination & Assessment

A multi-layered, continuous assessment system that keeps students consistently prepared and helps teachers identify and address learning gaps promptly.

Examination Type Frequency Classes Format Purpose
Unit Tests Monthly All Classes Written Track chapter-wise learning and reinforce concepts regularly.
Quarterly Examinations September All Classes Written Comprehensive test of Term 1 syllabus; results shared with parents.
Half-Yearly Examinations December All Classes Written Mid-year evaluation covering all subjects up to end of Term 2.
Pre-Final / Revision Exams February Classes 6–10 Written Full-syllabus rehearsal in actual exam conditions; confidence building.
SSC Board Preparation Tests Weekly (Jan–Mar) Classes 9 & 10 Board Format Intensive SSC mock tests replicating exact board exam pattern and timing.
Oral Assessments & Projects Each Term All Classes Oral / Project Evaluate comprehension, communication skills, and creative thinking.
"Our assessment system is designed not to judge students, but to guide them — helping every child understand where they stand and what steps will take them forward." — Academic Committee, Sri Kakatiya High School
