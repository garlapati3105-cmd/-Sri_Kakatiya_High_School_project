School Overview

A Premier Institution of Learning

Sri Kakatiya High School is a trusted educational institution nestled in Bagyanagar Colony, Shadnagar, Rangareddy. For over 15 years, we have been committed to delivering quality education that goes beyond textbooks — shaping character, building confidence, and nurturing a love for lifelong learning.

We follow the Telangana State Board SSC curriculum, offering classes from Nursery through Class X in both Telugu and English mediums. Our school is a place where every child is seen, heard, and encouraged to reach their fullest potential.

With state-of-the-art smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, a rich library, and dedicated sports facilities — we provide an environment that supports academic excellence alongside holistic development.

Our History

Our Journey of Excellence

From humble beginnings to a thriving institution — the story of Sri Kakatiya High School.

2009
Foundation

Sri Kakatiya High School was established in Bagyanagar Colony, Shadnagar, with a vision to provide quality education to children of the region. We began with Nursery and Primary classes and a committed team of educators.

2012
Expansion to High School

Responding to community demand, we expanded our offerings to include Classes 6 through 10 under the Telangana State Board SSC curriculum, enabling complete school education under one roof.

2015
Smart Classroom Initiative

Invested in digital infrastructure — installing interactive smart boards in every classroom to bring modern, engaging learning experiences to our students and improve academic outcomes.

2018
Science & Computer Labs

Inaugurated fully equipped Science and Computer laboratories, enabling hands-on practical learning and digital literacy for students from Class 5 onwards.

2021
800+ Student Community

Reached a milestone of over 800 enrolled students, reflecting the growing trust of families across Shadnagar and surrounding areas in the quality of Sri Kakatiya's education.

2026
Continuing Excellence

Today, Sri Kakatiya High School continues to grow — with enhanced facilities, dedicated faculty, and an unwavering commitment to nurturing every child's unique potential. Admissions open for 2026–27.
